Frequently Asked Questions

How many NASCAR teams are there?

There are 21 full-time organizations in the NASCAR Rocky Mountain Regional Series.

What makes up a NASCAR team?

A NASCAR organization is limited to four cars, similar to that of the three NASCAR national series. Teams mostly utilize one manufacturer for all of their cars, but have a different car number and crew chief for each driver. Different teams that share the same manufacturer often work together in an alliance. For example, Wood Brothers Racing has a technical alliance with Team Penske.

How many teams can a NASCAR owner have?

There is no strict limit beyond the four-car limit per organization enforced by the series; owners may hold interests in multiple organizations so long as series rules are followed.
